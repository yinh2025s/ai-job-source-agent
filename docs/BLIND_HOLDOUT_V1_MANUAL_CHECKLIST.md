# Blind Holdout V1 Manual Checklist

This checklist contains all 36 non-exact records from the frozen V1 live execution. It does
not change the frozen results or assign review labels.

For each record, check the LinkedIn source and official public pages, then record one outcome:

- `exact_exists`: an official, publicly accessible opening matches company/hiring entity,
  provider tenant, title, and location;
- `verified_closed_or_no_public`: the source is closed or complete official inventory has no
  matching public opening;
- `recruiter_client_undisclosed`: the visible company is a recruiter and the client cannot be
  established;
- `external_blocked`: login, CAPTCHA, bot protection, or another external barrier prevents a
  conclusion;
- `system_gap`: an eligible official opening exists, but the pipeline missed or rejected it;
- `eligibility_unknown`: public evidence is insufficient to decide.
